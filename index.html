About

Built by BlackFish

FileBox is a product of BlackFish Creative, dedicated to building simple, privacy-focused tools that respect your data. We believe that file processing shouldn't require uploading your sensitive documents to unknown servers.

All our tools run entirely in your browser using modern JavaScript APIs. Your files stay on your device, and we never see, store, or access any of your data.
